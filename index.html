The constraint

Credibility was there. The conversion path wasn't built.

Each company had a real product and a founder worth following. But LinkedIn wasn't structured to convert — content was inconsistent, outbound was manual and disconnected, and nothing tied posts, ICP targeting, and replies into a single motion that ended in booked calls.

  • Founder profiles weren't built to convert visitors into conversations
  • Content drove impressions, but no leads were tracked
  • Outbound ran from its own list, disconnected from content
  • No ICP scraping feeding a repeatable outreach motion
  • Nothing measured prospect → reply → call → close
The brief

Build one repeatable system — founder content, ICP scraping, LinkedIn + email outbound, reply-guy commenting, and conversion tracking — that turns attention into booked calls and qualified leads, without hiring a single new SDR.

The work

Three companies. Each measured by the metric that mattered.

One ran the full content-to-call system. One built authority for a raise. One turned content into inbound leads. Each carries the number that defined success.

The system

What we built

Five connected blocks, built to convert. The unit of work is the account and the founder's authority — measured all the way to booked calls, never a single isolated channel.

01

Founder positioning

  • Rewrote founder positioning
  • Clarified headline + profile message
  • Complex offers → simple narratives
  • Profiles built to convert pre-outreach
02

Content engine

  • Founder-led posts
  • Angles: pain points, trends, product, proof
  • Warms the market & drives inbound
  • Educational · opinion · proof · POV
3–4 founder posts/wk · weekly review
03

ICP scraping & targeting

  • Scraped, segmented ICP lists
  • Prioritized by role, type, relevance, intent
  • Split by persona + message angle
  • Founders · CEOs · Growth · BD · Investors
04

Outbound + reply-guy

  • LinkedIn DM sequences
  • Short, human first messages
  • Email outbound layered on top
  • Reply-guy + strategic commenting
~30 DMs/day · email · commenting · 3–5 touchpoints each
05

Conversion tracking

  • Prospect → reply → call → close
  • Positive-reply & call-booked rates
  • Best angles · best personas · inbound vs outbound
  • Objections + lost reasons logged
Weekly review · weekly iteration · monthly ICP refinement
